Frequently Asked Questions

Is RecruitAI free for professionals? ▼

Yes! RecruitAI is 100% free for candidates. Our mission is to empower professionals, and that includes free access to our platform and AI Career Co-Pilot.

How is my privacy protected as a candidate? ▼

Your profile is anonymous until you decide to accept a connection request from a company. You have full control over who sees your information and when.

What makes RecruitAI different from other job boards? ▼

We are not a job board. We are a talent marketplace. Instead of you applying for jobs, companies apply to you. This flipped model, combined with our powerful AI matching, results in higher quality connections and less wasted time.

What kind of companies hire on RecruitAI? ▼

We partner with a wide range of South Africa's most innovative companies, from fast-growing startups to established industry leaders across all sectors.
